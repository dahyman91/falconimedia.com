Sugar by Carmen Borla

Carmen Borla is an Argentinian artist whose life is devoted to telling stories. Through her acting, singing and writing she focuses on digging deep into the human experience.  She is a graduate from The New School (BFA) and AMDA (Musical Theatre) and also participated in the workshop BARS (Public Theatre).  Carmen believes expression is a form of processing experiences and exercising our empathy. Carmen is inspired by artists who stay in a constant state of curiosity.
